Frequently asked by our community – answered with warmth.

How do I store raw honey?
Keep it at room temperature in a sealed glass jar. Avoid refrigeration – crystallization is natural and delicious!
Do you ship internationally?
Currently we ship across USA and Canada. For other regions, please contact our team for a custom quote.
Can I return a product?
Absolutely – we offer a 30-day happiness guarantee. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund or replace.
