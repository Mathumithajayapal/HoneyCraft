Our Story

From a single hive to a flourishing sanctuary

Founded in 2015 by third-generation beekeeper Elara Wren, GoldenHive began with 12 rescued hives in Sonoma County. Today we steward over 400 hives across regenerative wildflower meadows, ensuring every drop of honey echoes the terroir of California's pristine landscapes.

Our mission bridges tradition and innovation — solar-powered extraction, no chemical treatments, and habitat corridors for native pollinators.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To nurture thriving pollinator ecosystems by practicing ethical, transparent beekeeping — delivering raw, unprocessed honey that preserves nature's purity while empowering local communities with regenerative agriculture education.

Our Vision

A future where every harvest strengthens the bond between people and pollinators, creating a global network of carbon-negative apiaries, thriving wildflower corridors, and a world where honey tells the story of ecological harmony.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped our apiary and impact

2015

First Hive, First Drop

GoldenHive founded with 12 rescued hives in Sonoma, starting the journey of ethical honey production.

2017

Regenerative Expansion

Expanded to 200+ hives & partnered with organic farms to create pollinator corridors across 500 acres.

2019

Solar-Powered Extraction

Launched first solar-powered honey extraction facility, reducing carbon footprint by 70%.

2022

Community Beekeeping School

Opened education center, training 300+ new beekeepers and donating 5% of profits to pollinator protection.

2025

Carbon-Negative Apiary Network

Achieved net-negative carbon status & protected 2,000+ acres of wildflower habitats.

From the Hive to Your Home

Our philosophy is simple: pure ingredients, ethical practices, and uncompromising quality

Natural Ingredients & Apothecary Craft

Our products celebrate the full bounty of the hive. Every balm, salve, and tincture begins with raw, unprocessed honey and golden beeswax, enhanced with organic botanical extracts we source from regenerative farms.

🐝 Raw Honey 🌿 Organic Lavender 🌼 Calendula 🍯 Propolis Extract 🌸 Rosehip Oil

Our making approach: Small-batch, cold-process formulations. We never use synthetic preservatives, artificial fragrances, or harsh emulsifiers. Each recipe is developed over months of testing to ensure synergy between honey's humectant properties and the healing power of botanicals.

Our Product Philosophy

We believe what you put on your body matters as much as what you put in it. That's why every GoldenHive apothecary product is:

  • 100% plastic-neutral & compostable packaging
  • Never tested on animals (Leaping Bunny certified)
  • Made in small batches for freshness & potency
  • Infused with raw, unheated honey for maximum enzymatic benefits
  • Blended with organic, fair-trade botanicals whenever possible

From our signature Honey Lip Repair to the Propolis Throat Spray, each product is a testament to the belief that nature, when respected, provides everything we need.

Why Beeswax? Nature's Golden Gift

The overlooked treasure of the hive — and why we celebrate it

Cleaner, Longer, Brighter

Unlike paraffin or soy wax candles, pure beeswax candles burn with a warm, naturally honeyed scent while releasing negative ions that purify indoor air. They burn brighter, longer, and drip very little — creating a meditative, toxin-free ambiance.

  • Hypoallergenic & non-toxic
  • Naturally dripless when tapered correctly
  • Emits a subtle, soothing honey aroma

Skin-Loving Benefits

Beeswax is a humectant and emollient — it draws moisture to the skin while creating a breathable protective barrier. In our balms and salves, beeswax locks in hydration without clogging pores, making it ideal for dry, cracked, or sensitive skin.

Why we choose beeswax over synthetics: It's naturally antibacterial, anti-inflammatory, and rich in vitamin A. From diaper balms to beard waxes, beeswax has been trusted for centuries — and we're proud to continue that tradition in every jar.
