Golden wisdom

Your questions about honey, wellness, and our craft — answered naturally.

Raw Honey & Purity
What makes GoldenHive honey "raw" and unfiltered?
Our honey is never heated above hive temperature and is only strained to remove large particles, preserving natural enzymes, pollen, and antioxidants. It's liquid gold in its purest form.
Why does my honey naturally crystallize?
Crystallization is a sign of high-quality raw honey! Simply place the jar in warm water (below 40°C) to restore silky texture without losing beneficial properties.
Do you test for pesticides or antibiotics?
Absolutely. Every batch undergoes third-party testing to ensure it's glyphosate-free, antibiotic-free, and meets our pure apiary standards.
Shipping & Delivery
How quickly will my order ship?
Orders are processed within 24-48 hours. Standard shipping takes 3-6 business days, and we offer express 2-day delivery for honey emergencies!
Do you ship glass jars safely in summer?
Yes! Our packaging includes protective insulation and climate‑conscious materials to keep honey safe from heat and breakage.
Can I track my hive-to-home package?
Every shipment includes a tracking link via email/SMS. Plus, you'll receive a "bee-utiful" surprise sample in your first order.
Wellness & Everyday Use
What are the health benefits of raw honey?
Rich in antioxidants, antibacterial properties, soothes sore throats, supports gut health, and can be a natural pre-workout energy source.
Is GoldenHive honey safe for toddlers?
We recommend honey only for children over 12 months due to the rare risk of infant botulism. For adults and kids >1 year, it's a wholesome treat.
Can I use honey on my skin?
Definitely! Our raw honey is perfect for DIY face masks, healing minor burns, or mixing into bath soaks. It's nature's humectant.
Ethical Apiaries & Sustainability
How do you practice ethical beekeeping?
We follow natural hive management: no queen wing clipping, leave enough honey for bees, avoid synthetic treatments, and prioritize pollinator habitats.
Are your hives organic certified?
We work with small organic farms and follow regenerative agriculture protocols. While we're in transition for full certification, you can trace every jar's origin.
How does GoldenHive support wild bees?
5% of profits go to pollinator protection programs and planting wildflower corridors. We also donate hives to educational nonprofits.
Beeswax Apothecary Care
Why choose beeswax candles over soy or paraffin?
Beeswax burns cleaner, emits negative ions that purify air, lasts longer, and has a subtle honey aroma. No toxins, just golden glow.
How to clean beeswax wraps / balm tins?
For wraps: rinse with cold water & mild soap — avoid heat. Balm tins can be wiped clean with a soft cloth; reuse infinitely.
Are your skincare products safe for sensitive skin?
Yes! Our balms & salves are made with 3-5 organic ingredients: beeswax, raw honey, and cold-pressed oils. No synthetic fragrances or preservatives.
